How to Play

1. Pick your favorite lynx avatar.

2. Press SPACE or click Start Game to begin.

3. Find the matching cat on the grid.

4. Click the cat and press ENTER to confirm.

5. You lose a life if you're wrong or run out of time.

6. Reach 10 points to win.
